UX Design Project

AIB Linkin' Mobile App ‚Üó

For the first phase of the UX/UI Bootcamp, we were tasked with leveraging the Design Process to create a Mobile App that would help someone in their day-to-day life within a three week timeline. As we were in the beginning months of the COVID-19 Pandemic, it became apparent that it was causing a lot of distress among Millennials and Gen-Z -- a group commonly overlooked and unaware of how to get the help that they need.


UX/UI Design Project

NSA Website Redesign ‚Üó

As part of the second phase of the UX/UI Bootcamp, I was tasked to redesign a Government website. Given the websites available, I selected the National Security Agency (NSA). As the focus of this project was the UI Design, I performed enough user research to get a very broad understanding of who the primary user may be so I could tailor the UI to appeal to them.


UX/UI Design + Dev

AFKA Website Redesign ‚Üó

For third phase of the UX/UI Bootcamp, we were tasked to choose an existing website to redesign and take all the way through development in three weeks. We chose the American Forest Kindergarden Association website because we felt that due to the Pandemic and the prevalence of remote learning, people would be seeking non-traditional methods of schooling for their children.
